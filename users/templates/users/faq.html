How works this page?

Our webpage is a place where people can buy/sell keys, simply as that.

Is this legal?

Totally, once you own a key you can do whatever you want to with it.

How is this profitable?

Our page takes a fee of the product, the seller probably bought bundles of keys with a proper discount or from countrys with lower taxes so he gets a slice of the cake too, meanwhile the client gets a juicy cheap game

How can I buy a digital product on G2A.COM?

First of all, you need to create an account on this page, keep reading.

How do I search for digital products on G2A.COM?

First, take a look on what you want, if you know what game you want to buy, you can specifically search it on the search bar or scrolling on the home page.

I found what I want to buy, what do I do now?

Now that you’ve found what you were looking for, you should check the details of the product to make sure that you're getting the right version for the right platform. For now we only offer keys for steam on EU, so you should have that in mind beforing buying.

All set? Awesome! Now, just add the product to your cart.

You can either pick the Selected Offer (just click on the blue "Add to cart" button) or scroll down for a list of available offers where you can choose the best one by clicking the blue cart button right next to it.

Each seller has a few details that you can check about him as well like their rating, the more rating he/she has the more trustworthy

Once you've decided on the offer, you can either continue shopping or go to checkout to complete the purchase.

Can I now when the delivery arrives?

Since we are working with virtual data, you'll get the digital product you bought as soon as your order is processed and completed.

I’ve added the products to my cart, how do I complete my purchase?

You're almost there! Time to check out your cart's contents if you added all that you needed. Simply click on the shopping cart you see right above all.

You will see the items you added and their quantity (for now limited to 1) and the seller(s) you're buying the product(s) from

If everything is fine, you can checkout and complete de payment. All you need is wait for the payment to be processed. This may take from a few seconds to a couple of minutes. Once it's done, you will get the products you bought as soon as possible!

I paid for my order, is there anything else I should do?

When the payment finish your purchase, you'll be redirected to your profile, where you can click on 'Keys' to see your keys.

I didn't receive any or every product in my order. What should I do?

You’ve made a purchase, but an item is missing? If that’s the case, the seller most likely ran out of keys right during the finalization of your order, which is why you received only a part of it. Worry not - If this happens, we will talk with the seller Contact us so we are aware of your problem on gamingwebapp@gmail.com
